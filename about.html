Natalia Costa

Hi! I am a MBA and MSI student at the University of Michigan.
Born and raised in Brazil, I majored in International Relations with a minor in Business and Marketing. I worked for 5 year at Procter & Gamble, in São Paulo, with Marketing Communications, eventually being promoted to Communications Manager for Gillette before moving to the United States for my dual masters.

Both my undergrad and previous professional experience made me realize how passionate I am about human behavior and my current degrees are geared toward further leveraging such understanding as a positive force in business. I am especially interested in the advances of voice interfaces and their impact on accessibility.

On a personal level, I am all about learning new languages, good food, great music (ecletic but with a soft spot for classic rock), and lots of quality time with my loved ones.
